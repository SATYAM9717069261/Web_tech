Welcome to Canteen Management System

Many organizations provide Canteen as an additional subsidized facility to their Employees as a welfare measure. The usage of the canteen by the employees depends on many factors like centralized food distribution center with swift and Simplicity in operations. CMS ( Canteen Management System ) design provides a user friendly system that facilitates quick and efficient operations to cover larger section of employees within a specified time. CMS is paperless and has almost cashless transactions. This system is also compatible in an environment where multiple Caterers operate in a closed environment or at multiple locations
